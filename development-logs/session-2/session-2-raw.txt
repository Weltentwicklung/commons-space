**"Arbiter"** means a person who settles disputes or makes judgments between conflicting parties.

In this context, it refers to someone who:
- Resolves conflicts
- Makes decisions in disputes
- Acts as a judge or mediator
- Provides objective judgment when communities are in crisis

**The key point:** People might serve as arbiters temporarily, but should NOT:
- Make it their career
- Build their livelihood around this role
- Become professional "conflict resolvers"

**Example from Session 1:**
When a village has an emotional crisis, they might call objective judges from neighboring communities. These judges serve temporarily, then the structure dissolves.
